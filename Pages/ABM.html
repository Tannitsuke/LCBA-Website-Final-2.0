Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM)

ABM, which stands for Accountancy, Business, and Management, is one of LCBA’s Senior High School (SHS) programs offered under the Academic Track. The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Strand equips students with essential knowledge and skills in business, finance, and entrepreneurship. It helps learners develop strong leadership, decision-making, and management abilities, preparing them for future careers in accounting, business administration, marketing, banking, and other business-related fields.

This strand is best for students who are interested in:

Business and entrepreneurship

Accounting and finance

Marketing and management

Economics and
corporate operations

What You Learn in ABM

  • Fundamentals of Accounting, Business, and Management
  • Business Mathematics
  • Applied Economics
  • Principles of Marketing
  • Business Ethics and Social Responsibility
  • Entrepreneurship

Possible College Courses After ABM

ABM is a great foundation if you plan to take:

Accountancy

Business Administration

Financial Management

Marketing Management

Entrepreneurship

Economics

Future Careers

ABM students often pursue careers such as:

Accountant

Business owner / Entrepreneur

Marketing officer

Bank or finance employee

Manager or corporate professional
